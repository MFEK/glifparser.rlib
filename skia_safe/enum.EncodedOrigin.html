Expand description

These values match the orientation www.exif.org/Exif2-2.PDF.

Variants

Default

TopRight

Reflected across y-axis

BottomRight

Rotated 180

BottomLeft

Reflected across x-axis

Reflected across x-axis, Rotated 90 CCW

Rotated 90 CW

Reflected across x-axis, Rotated 90 CW
